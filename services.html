ELEVATOR MAINTENANCE

Careful and consistent maintenance improves the long-term reliability, safety and efficiency of your elevators. At Broward Elevator & Lift, we will maintain your elevators on a monthly basis, including cleaning and lubricating parts, testing the telephone and alarm bell and examining all elevator equipment to ensure it is safe and operating properly.

Our full-service maintenance contract guarantees that you pay one monthly fee in exchange for monthly maintenance of your elevators PLUS service calls and repairs. If an elevator breaks down and needs service between monthly visits, we make the repairs at no additional cost to you.

Insuring your elevators with a full-service maintenance contract means you avoid the expense of major elevator repairs, as well as the liability of accidents.

We maintain all types of elevator equipment for all makes and models of elevators, including those manufactured by the major elevator companies: Otis, Schindler, ThyssenKrupp and KONE.

Broward Elevator & Lift offers both a full-service maintenance contract, as well as a limited maintenance contract for residential and commercial customers in Palm Beach, Broward, Miami Dade, and Monroe County in South Florida.

ELEVATOR UPGRADE AND MODERNIZATION

If you’ve considered upgrading or modernizing your elevators but haven’t taken the leap yet, ask yourself these questions:

  • Do your elevators break down often?
  • Do they look old and outdated?
  • Do they comply with current code and safety standards?
  • Have passengers complained about the wait time, ride quality or strange noises?

If you answered “yes” to any of these, it’s time to modernize. One benefit of elevator modernization is improving the performance, appearance and safety of your elevators. Another benefit of modernization is that it keeps older buildings competitive with newer buildings and increases the value of your building. For these reasons, elevator modernization typically provides a good return on investment.

Broward Elevator & Lift specializes in elevator upgrades and modernization. Whether you’re looking to make a few cosmetic changes or you want to completely strip out and replace the elevator interior and/or equipment, Broward Elevator & Lift can perform your elevator upgrade or modernization. What’s more, we can usually do the job at a much lower cost than our competitors!

Major elevator modifications typically only happen every 25 to 30 years. That’s why it’s critically important to do it right. Based on a full inspection of your elevator equipment, Broward Elevator & Lift will develop a detailed analysis and recommendations for what you should include in your modernization project. Having managed multi-million dollar modernization projects for large elevator companies, you can be assured we have the knowledge and experience to perform the job on time and within budget.

If you’re ready to enjoy the benefits of a new elevator without the expense, contact Broward Elevator & Lift today. Call us at 954-491-8161 or contact us to analyze and quote your modernization project.

ELEVATOR SAFETY TESTS AND CERTIFICATION

Is your elevator certified or up to code? The State of Florida requires that all elevators meet certain safety criteria (telephone, door locking mechanism, proper Braille, door protection, etc). Broward Elevator & Lift is your local authority regarding the requirements to have your elevator annually certified in Palm Beach, Broward, Miami Dade and Monroe County. We always stay updated about the latest changes, and we’re highly experienced in getting customers through the inspection and certification renewal process quickly and on time.

The following safety tests are required by the State of Florida and included in our full-service maintenance contract:

  • Annual No load Governor Safety Tests for Traction Elevators (1002.1 A17.1).
  • Five-Year Full Load Governor Safety Tests for Traction Elevators (1002.3 A17.1).
  • Annual Hydraulic Pressure Test for Hydraulic elevators (1004 A17.1).
  • Normal and Final Terminal Stopping Devices (1002.2E A17.1).
  • Oil Buffer Test (1002.2A A17.1).
  • Power Operation of Door System (1002.2H A17.1).

In Florida, recent changes in the law require certain repairs and upgrades be made to elevators to comply with current codes and safety standards. Broward Elevator & Lift will be happy to inspect your elevator, provide an estimate and perform these repairs for those who do not have an elevator maintenance contract.

To find out if your elevator needs repairs to bring it up to code, or if you need help with the elevator certification process, call Broward Elevator & Lift at 954-491-8161 or contact us today.
