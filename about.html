About Us

Broward Elevator & Lift fuses the knowledge and experience of a big corporation with the service of a small business. Whether you have a single two-story building with one elevator or a dozen multi-story buildings with multiple elevators, we provide all our customers the same fast, friendly, personal service.

At Broward Elevator & Lift, we provide honest, reliable, individualized elevator service and repair at an affordable price. All of our elevator technicians have years of experience and are certified by the state of Florida. They are experts in maintaining, repairing and upgrading all makes and models of elevators, as well as installing custom-engineered elevators in new or existing buildings.

In addition to elevators, Broward Elevator & Lift installs and services wheelchair lifts, home lifts, stair chairs and dumbwaiters in residential and commercial buildings throughout South Florida, including Palm Beach, Broward, Miami Dade and Monroe County.

Your satisfaction and the safety of your building’s elevators is our top priority. We will ensure that your elevators are in top working order so you don’t have to worry about it.

Need an estimate for an elevator service maintenance contract, repair job, modernization project or custom installation? Do you need a wheelchair lift, home lift, stair chair or dumbwaiter installed or serviced? Call Broward Elevator & Lift today. We do what we promise at a price you can afford. We look forward to hearing from you and earning your business.
